What is the Gospel Genius Extension?

General Conference talks from the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints feature extensive footnotes to scriptures. These footnotes are meant to inspire deeper study of these words from modern prophets. However, scriptures on the Church's website currently have no references to relevant General Conference talks. The ability to jump to related teachings from modern prophets when reading the scriptures would deepen study and build faith. The Gospel Genius extension aims to do just that!

The Gospel Genius replaces the verse numbers on the Church's website with buttons. These buttons display the verse number and the number of times that verse has appeared as a footnote in General Conference talks. Clicking on the verse buttons will display a table with links to each talk that references the verse. The color of the verse buttons can be customized in the Gospel Genius settings window. If desired, the settings window also enables the ability to filter the tables and footnote counts to only General Conference talks from Apostles.


How Does the Gospel Genius Extension Gather Footnote Data?

The Gospel Genius runs a webscraping utility after the release of new General Conference talks biannually. The Gospel Genius searches for links to scriptures within every Conference Talk - both new and previous - to maintain the most up to data footnote data. After the scripture links are extracted they are decoded using functions to create a usable dataset. These datasets can be found here on my website's Datasets Page.


How Can the Gospel Genius Help Me Study Come Follow Me?

Come Follow Me is an excellent scripture study resource provided by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. These study outlines help members to read and ponder one of the Four Standard Works every year. Lessons include insights on scripture passages, prompts for gospel topic discussions, and ideas for family activities that assist in the learning process. The Gospel Genius Extension can help to make this scripture study even more meaningful!

The Gospel Genius provides members with links to relevant General Conference talks built into the current week's readings. The hard to understand passages from Isaiah and Revelation will become easier when combines with the words of General Conference talks. Relevant Conference Talks provide additional study and family activities through the week. The Gospel Genius create links between Come Follow Me and General Conference talks!
